Correlation & Causation

Correlation: Two vaiables are correlated when knowing the value of one gives you information about the likely value of the other.

Relation: Two states are casually related, when one state influences the otehr via cause and effect process.

"Spurious" correlation: somehow two things correlated purely by chance.


Summary:

When you see a correlation,

  • 1. Coincidence?
  • 2. A --> B or B --> A (causatoin direction)
  • 3. Does it have multiple causes? What is the most important cause?
  • 4. Do they have common cause?
  • 5. It is cause necessary, sufficient, or contributory?
